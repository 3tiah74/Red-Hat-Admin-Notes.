### **Minimum System Requirements for RHEL 9**

- Working network connection  
- Installation media  
- Dual or quad-core processor  
- 2 GB of RAM or more  
- 20 GB of disk space or more  

**Disk Partitions:**

- 10 GB for `/root`  
- 1 GB for Swap  
- 4 GB for `/home`  
- 512 MB for `/boot`
